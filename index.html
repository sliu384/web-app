Drawing Tablets

Why would I want one?

Why would you want a drawing tablet, you say? Well, a drawing tablet, besides drawing, can perform many other things, like editing videos, photos, and other various forms of media. Mostly, many people buy these tablets as a way to draw online, as a way of imitating the way pencil and paper work. A digital canvas, along with the app, provides much more than a basic pencil and paper can; allowing for an easier workflow.
